- Wait 10 seconds

- Upload your Webull or TradingView screenshot
- Get your signal!

TO STOP THE TOOL:
- Double-click STOP.bat

REQUIREMENTS:
- Python must be installed
- Node.js must be installed
- Tesseract OCR must be installed
